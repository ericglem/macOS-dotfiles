Enable Browser Toolbox
  1. Press Option+Command+I
  2. Click on the three dots next to the close button on the top right corner
  3. Click on 'Settings'
  4. Scroll to 'Advanced settings'
  5. Check the settings 'Enable browser chrome and add-on debugging toolboxes'
     and 'Enable remote debugging'

Get the name of a CSS Selector
  1. Press Shift+Option+Command+I to open the Browser Toolbox
  2. Allow incoming connection
  3. To select a box press the button in the top left corner (next to the
     'Inspector tab')
  4. To select popup menus click on the three dots on the top right and check
     'Disable Poput Auto-Hide'
  5. Click on what you want to select
  6. Right click the highlighted text, then -> Copy -> CSS Selector

Inspect a box's layout (height, widht, margins, paddings & borders)
  1. Repeat previous steps 1-6
  2. Click the 'Layout' tab (between the 'Rules' and 'Computed' tabs on the
     right side of the window)
  3. Scroll down to 'Box Model'
